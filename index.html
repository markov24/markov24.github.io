About me

I am broadly interested in the foundations of machine learning, with specific interests in causality and deep learning theory.

Before Michigan, I completed a double major in computer science and mathematics at Northwestern University, where I worked on projects spanning robust machine learning, causal inference in natural language processing, and efficient transfer learning for large language models.
